Scaffold `apps/web` — the first `apps/*` workspace — as a Next.js 16 App-Router shell wired into the existing npm-workspace monorepo, and stand up the load-bearing build/boundary scaffold the rest of the phase depends on: `transpilePackages` for the raw-TS workspace packages, the `server-only` container singleton, the `apps/web` Vitest project, and the eslint boundary guards that keep `@house/app`/`better-sqlite3` out of the client bundle and confine float conversion to the display edge.
