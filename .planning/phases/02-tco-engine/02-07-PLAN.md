Add `ScenarioInputsSchema` (Zod) mirroring `assumptions/schema.ts` (dollar/rate fields as `decStr`, counts as positive integers, `downPaymentPct` in [0,1), `.strict()`), parse through it in `engineInput()` (and expose `parseScenarioInputs` for the snapshot loader), replace the bare cast at `golden.test.ts` round-trip, and export the schema/parse fn from the barrel.

Purpose: this is itself a STRIDE Tampering mitigation — every TCO requirement (TCO-01..TCO-07) depends on a well-formed scenario; the calc must reject forged inputs at the door, not silently produce a negative loan or an empty schedule.

Output: a validating `engine-input.ts` (`ScenarioInputsSchema` + `parseScenarioInputs`), boundary tests, a real-parse round-trip in `golden.test.ts`, and the new exports.
</objective>
