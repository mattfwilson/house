Build the flagship-enabling **two-portfolio rent-vs-buy** engine (TCO-07) and close the **reproducibility loop** for the full TCO result. Over `holdingYears`, project two symmetric net-worth trajectories — BUY (home equity = forced-savings principal + conservative separate appreciation, liquidated with the explicit sell haircut) vs RENT (invest the down payment + closing at t=0 plus the monthly difference) — both compounding at the REAL return via the Fisher relation. Output ending net worth per path + the crossover year, and prove at least one realistic input set lets RENT win (anti-funnel). Then extend the golden harness with a committed TCO fixture and regenerate the existing canary fixture broken by the Plan 01 shape change.

It is the gating defense against opportunity-cost asymmetry (Pitfall 6) and naive real-vs-nominal (Pitfall 5). The golden fixture proves the whole TCO result is deterministic before persistence (Phase 6) exists.

Output: New `tco/rent-vs-buy.ts` + tests, extended `golden.test.ts` + committed `tco-golden-snapshot.json`, regenerated `golden-snapshot.json`, and updated public barrel.
