<objective>
Build the three independent per-line cost calculators of the TCO breakdown: **property tax** (TCO-02 — assessed x mill rate, appreciating assessed at constant rate, the Prop 2.5 flag), **carrying costs** (TCO-03 — maintenance on appreciating value, flat insurance, flat HOA), and **closing costs** (TCO-05 — %-of-price or dollar override, amortizable over the hold). All do rate math in `Dec` and surface dollars as `Money`.

Purpose: These line items feed the monthly+annualized breakdown `computeTco` assembles in Plan 04. Property tax is a gating correctness pitfall (Pitfall 9 — Prop 2.5 is a levy cap, not a bill cap; tax must never be a flat % or a 2.5%-clamped bill). This plan runs in parallel with Plan 02 (disjoint files), both depending only on Plan 01's widened assumptions + town table.

Output: New `tco/property-tax.ts`, `tco/carrying-costs.ts`, `tco/closing-costs.ts` with full unit test suites.
</objective>
