<objective>
Build the two tightly-coupled correctness cores of the TCO engine: the **fixed-rate amortization schedule** (TCO-01) and **PMI** (TCO-04). PMI reads the scheduled amortized balance per period, so the two ship together. Both do rate/power/division math in the internal `Dec` clone and surface dollars only as `Money` (the sanctioned `canary.ts` precedent), and both are proven by exact-equality invariant tests — never `toBeCloseTo`.

Purpose: These are the existential correctness pitfalls of the phase (PITFALLS 2 and 3). The amortization schedule's exact-$0 final balance + principal-sum invariants and the PMI 78/80-original-value mechanics are the gating proofs SC1 and SC3 demand. Downstream `computeTco` (Plan 04) and `rentVsBuy` (Plan 05) consume the schedule and the per-period PMI.

Output: New `tco/amortization.ts` + `tco/pmi.ts` with full invariant/toggle test suites including the external-oracle non-round-rate case.
</objective>
